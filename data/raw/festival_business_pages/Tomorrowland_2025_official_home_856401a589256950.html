History

Tomorrowland since 2005

What started in 2005 as a small gathering in Boom, Belgium, has grown into one of the most iconic and beloved music festivals in the world. Tomorrowland’s history is a story of passion, innovation, and connection – uniting hundreds of thousands of People of Tomorrow from across the globe. With each edition, the festival continues to push creative boundaries and create unforgettable moments that live on forever in the hearts of those who experience it.
